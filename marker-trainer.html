Save these files in a folder called markers/[your-entry-id]/ next to your journal.html. The app will look for them there.

How it works

  1. Upload the exact photo you'll print for this journal entry.
  2. Give it the same ID you used (or plan to use) in the journal app.
  3. Download the three generated files and place them at markers/[id]/marker (the app expects marker.fset, marker.fset3, marker.iset).
  4. In the journal app's Manage Entries screen, make sure the entry ID matches exactly.
  5. When you point your camera at the printed photo, the app will recognize it and play the video.
